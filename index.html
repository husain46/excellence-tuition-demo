Our Story

Founded in 2019 in the heart of Business Bay, Excellence Tuition Centre was built on one simple belief: every student can achieve outstanding results when they receive the right guidance, in the right environment, at the right time.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between classroom learning and exam excellence. We work with students across IGCSE, A Levels, IB, and CBSE — providing structured, curriculum-aligned support that produces measurable grade improvements within weeks.

We keep our batch sizes deliberately small — a maximum of 6 students per group — so that every student receives meaningful individual attention. Our tutors are subject specialists who have themselves excelled in the examinations they now teach.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free trial class work?
WhatsApp us or fill in the contact form and we'll schedule a free 60-minute trial class at your preferred time. You pick the subject and curriculum. The tutor will run a mini assessment to understand your child's current level. No payment required and no obligation to continue.
How many students are in each batch?
We cap every batch at a maximum of 6 students. This ensures every student receives meaningful attention in every session. We also offer one-on-one sessions for students who prefer fully personalised instruction — contact us for dedicated pricing.
Do you offer online classes?
Yes. We offer fully live online classes via Zoom with digital whiteboard, screen sharing, and recorded sessions. Online students receive exactly the same curriculum support and WhatsApp access as in-person students.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Cash, Bank Transfer (UAE bank accounts), and Card payment. Monthly fees are due at the start of each month. We do not charge any registration or joining fee.
What if my child misses a class?
Missed classes can be rescheduled with 24 hours notice at no extra charge. We also send a session summary after every class so your child never falls behind on what was covered.
Can we switch subjects or curriculums midway?
Absolutely. If your child wants to add a subject, drop one, or switch curriculums mid-year, we accommodate that with no penalty. Just WhatsApp us and we'll arrange new sessions within 24 hours.
